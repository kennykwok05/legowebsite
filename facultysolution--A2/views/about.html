About

Everything you want to know about me
-the designer & developer-


My name is Kenny Kwok.

I was born on July 13, 2005, in Surabaya, Indonesia. I'm a third-semester student at Seneca College, where I am pursuing a degree in Computer Programming Analysis. My passion for coding and problem-solving began in high school when I took a coding course and realized how much I enjoyed it. This love for programming is what led me to choose my current field of study. Outside of academics, I have a diverse range of hobbies. I enjoy playing musical instruments such as the drums, piano, and guitar, and I have a deep appreciation for music, particularly J-pop and K-pop. I also love watching Anime and K-drama series, which provide a great escape and source of inspiration.
